# Outreach Templates

## Recruiter Message (<=120 words)
Hi <Name>, I applied for <Role> at <Company>. I have <X years> in <relevant area> and recently delivered <specific outcome with metric>. The role's focus on <requirement> aligns closely with my background in <proof>. If helpful, I can share a brief summary of relevant projects. Would you be open to a quick conversation?

## Hiring Manager Message (<=120 words)
Hi <Name>, I applied for <Role> and wanted to share why I'm a strong match. In my current/last role, I <action> resulting in <metric/result>. That experience maps directly to your need for <requirement>. I would value the chance to discuss how I can contribute to <team objective>. Thank you for your time.

## Follow-up Message (5-7 days)
Hi <Name>, following up on my application for <Role>. I remain very interested and wanted to re-share my relevant experience in <area>. Happy to provide additional details or work samples if useful.
